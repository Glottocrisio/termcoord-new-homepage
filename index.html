IATE, the EU’s interinstitutional terminology database was developed in the early 2000s.

 

The IATE Management Group meets several times a year; in these meetings the European Parliament is represented by TermCoord.

 

Termer gives users access to translations or meanings of words directly from the webpage. TERMER can automatically connect glossaries to online content to save time and to increase the lookup relevance with sources tailored to the content. The IATE demonstration on Termcoord is limited, and shows only the English to French entries, one of the more than 600 available language pairs.

 

IATE Public provides now the possibility to download TBX files filtered by languages and domains. Users can extract subsets of data as follows, using the extraction tool IATExtract.

The IATE Management Group (IMG) was created in 2009 by the Coordinating Committee on Translation (CCT) as part of the governance for interinstitutional IT tools.

 

It consists of the tool manager and a representative from each of the participating institutions/bodies and is responsible for coordinating content and development issues as well as joint activities.

 

For the cooperation on Terminology among the EU Institutions, the IATE Management Group created and manages an Interinstitutional Terminology Portal called EurTerm, which contains resources from all participating Institutions as well as collaborative platforms for the online cooperation in Terminology Projects

 

EurTerm is accessible only to all staff of the EU Institutions through an account secured by a password (EU login) and to some collaborating external experts to whom a read access can be granted.
